Out of all the places I have traveled in the world, there's no place like home and Minnesota persists as one of my favorite places ever! This article from City Pages encapsulates some of the reasons why: http://www.citypages.com/arts/50-reasons-minnesota-is-the-best-state-in-america-6584126
